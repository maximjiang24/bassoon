How to use this app
1
Practice Session — press Record, play your bassoon for 15–30 s. The app detects each note and scores its stability individually. Scales, long tones, and passages all work.
2
Targeted Exercises — the app identifies your weakest notes from past sessions and builds a short exercise set. Each exercise scores automatically.
3
Reed Journal — log each reed with response rating, intonation tendency, and lifecycle stage. Record the standardised scale to compare stability across reeds.
4
Progress — after 3 sessions your trend charts, register breakdown, and note heatmap unlock automatically. Keep practising and watch the data build.
